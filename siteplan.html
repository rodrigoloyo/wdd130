Site Name

The site name: Dry Oar

Site Purporse

The Dry Oar site will promote rafting tours for a local agency in Costa Rica, it will contain beautiful picture for rafting tours and inform about culture and wildlife, especially the Sloth as national symbol, with emphasis about Nature-friendly: we can coexist together in harmony and have fun!

Season’s promotion and discount. Drone option for the tour, instructors, guides, hotel information partners.
